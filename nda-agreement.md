# Confidentiality, Non-Compete and Non-Disclosure Agreement

This Confidentiality, Non-Compete and Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into this ____ day of _____________________, 20______, by and between Rising Orchards, LLC, a Florida limited liability company (referred to as "Rising Orchards"), and __________________________, having a principal address of ___________________________________ ________________________________ ("Receiving Party"), together Rising Orchards and Receiving Party are "the Parties".

**WHEREAS**, Rising Orchards is the owner of certain intellectual property in the form of inventions, systems, business models, training strategies, marketing, trademarks, formulas, formulations, trade secrets and other property related to the development, manufacture and commercialization of animated props and related technology;

**WHEREAS**, Rising Orchards may share Confidential Information, as hereinafter defined, with Receiving Party while exploring whether Receiving Party desires to engage in business transactions with Rising Orchards and may further share Confidential Information, as hereinafter defined, with Receiving Party if business transactions are commenced with Receiving Party; and;

**NOW THEREFORE**, as a result of and in consideration of the mutual promises and covenants contained herein and the disclosure of confidential information, the parties agree as follows:

**Confidential Information:** "Confidential Information" shall mean, without limitation, all non-public information relating to business plans or practices, financial or technical matters, formulas or formulations, trade secrets, designs, know-how, property, as previously defined, inventions, operations, the marketing or promotion of the property, business and information received from others that Rising Orchards considers confidential and any other information, whether in writing or orally, received or acquired by Receiving Party from Rising Orchards in the course of any business dealings between the parties. Confidential Information shall not include any information, however designated, that: (i) is or subsequently becomes publicly available through no wrongful act of Receiving Party; (ii) is already known to Receiving Party at the time of disclosure; (iii) is rightfully received by Receiving Party from a third party without restriction on disclosure and without breach of this Agreement; (iv) is independently developed by Receiving Party without the use of any of the Confidential Information;

**Limitations on Use:** Receiving Party agrees to accept Rising Orchard's Confidential Information solely for use in determining whether the Receiving Party will engage in business transactions with Rising Orchards and while conducting business with Rising Orchards, if applicable. Receiving Party should refrain from reverse engineering, decompiling or disassembling Confidential Information and not disclose, publish, distribute or disseminate Confidential Information to anyone other than those of its employees or consultants with a need to know in pursuance of possible or actual business transactions with Rising Orchards. Receiving Party agrees to make any employees or consultants who are provided Confidential Information aware of these restrictions on use and require their compliance. Receiving Party agrees to use reasonable care, but in no event less than the same degree of care that it uses to protect his, her or its own confidential and proprietary information of similar importance, to prevent the unauthorized use, disclosure, publication and dissemination of Confidential Information. Receiving Party agrees not to use Confidential Information otherwise for his, her or its own or any third party's benefit without the prior written approval of an authorized representative of Rising Orchards. Specifically, the parties contemplate disclosure and development of formulations and production of products and materials for exclusive use by the Parties, and Receiving Party agrees that he, she or it will not disclose, sell to or use the designs or products contemplated by this agreement with any other party without written permission from Rising Orchards.

**No Rights Granted:** The property, including, but not limited to, any computer code, technologies, formulas or formulations, trademarks, copyrighted materials, and any other property and Confidential Information of Rising Orchards, provided or otherwise utilized pursuant to this Agreement, is and will remain the property of Rising Orchards, and Receiving Party shall have no claim to ownership of such property, nor will Receiving Party use such property, except as expressly permitted by Rising Orchards, pursuant to this Agreement in conjunction with determining whether Receiving Party will engage in business transactions with Rising Orchards and while conducting business with Rising Orchards, if applicable, or as otherwise agreed in writing by the Parties. Nothing in this Agreement shall be construed as Rising Orchards granting Receiving Party any expressed or implied rights under any patent, copyright or other form of intellectual property, nor shall this Agreement grant either party any express or implied rights of Receiving Party to Rising Orchards' Confidential Information other than to provide services to Rising Orchards.

**Mandatory Disclosure Exemption:** Receiving Party may disclose Confidential Information in accordance with a judicial or other governmental order, provided that Receiving Party either (i) gives Rising Orchards reasonable notice prior to such disclosure to allow Rising Orchards to have a reasonable opportunity to seek a protective order or equivalent, or (ii) obtains written assurance from the applicable judicial or governmental entity that it will afford the Confidential Information the highest level of protection under applicable law or regulation.

**Return of Confidential Information:** Upon the first request of Rising Orchards, Receiving Party shall return all originals, copies, reproductions and summaries of all Confidential Information which were, at any time, in the possession of Receiving Party and all materials (in any medium or form) which contain or embody Confidential Information.

**Remedies:** Rising Orchards and Receiving Party both agree that their obligations set forth in this Agreement are necessary and reasonable in order to protect Rising Orchards and its business. Both parties expressly agree that due to the unique nature of Rising Orchards' Confidential Information, monetary damages would be inadequate to compensate Rising Orchards for any breach by Receiving Party of its covenants and agreements set forth in this Agreement. Accordingly, Rising Orchards and Receiving Party both agree and acknowledge that any such violation or threatened violation shall cause irreparable injury to Rising Orchards and that, in addition to any other remedies that may be available, in law, in equity or otherwise, Rising Orchards shall be entitled (a) to obtain injunctive/interdictory relief against the threatened breach of this Agreement or the continuation of any such breach by Receiving Party, without the necessity of proving actual damages, and (b) to be indemnified by Receiving Party from any loss or harm, including, without limitation, attorney's fees, arising out of or in connection with any breach or enforcement of Receiving Party's obligations under this Agreement or the unauthorized use or disclosure of Rising Orchards' Confidential Information.

**Non-Compete:** Receiving Party shall not, without receiving the prior express written consent of Rising Orchards, exploit the Confidential Information disclosed by Rising Orchards, including selling, or be involved with the development, manufacture and commercialization of animated props and related technology by exploiting the Confidential Information provided by Rising Orchards.

**Non-Solicitation:** Receiving Party agrees that for two years following the execution of this Agreement, Receiving Party will not hire or participate in the hiring of, directly or indirectly, any person who is employed by Rising Orchards, or who was employed by Rising Orchards during the twelve (12) months prior to the execution of this Agreement.

**Limitation of Liability:** Rising Orchards will not be liable to Receiving Party or to any other person whatsoever, for any loss, damage or claim occasioned by the acts or omissions of Receiving Party in using Confidential Information or intellection property in conjunction with contemplating or engaging in business transactions with Rising Orchards, as they may relate, or be alleged to relate, to this Agreement or any other relationship between Rising Orchards or such third party (referred to collectively as "claims"). Receiving Party will, therefore, indemnify and hold Rising Orchards harmless from and against any and all claims.

**Dispute Resolution and Attorney's Fees:** Should any dispute arise from this Agreement, the parties may require the dispute to be mediated in Volusia County, Florida prior to being arbitrated. If Rising Orchards elects not to so mediate, the dispute shall be resolved by final and binding arbitration in Volusia County, Florida. The manner in which mediation and arbitration will be conducted will be within the confines of applicable rules and law, except that any arbitration will be conducted pursuant to the AAA Rules for Commercial Arbitration. The parties agree to split the cost of any mediation, including any attorney's fees. The prevailing party in any proceedings other than mediation, including but not limited to arbitration, shall be entitled to attorney's fees and costs related to the proceedings, including attorney's fees related to a determination of the amount of attorney's fees owed including fees related to establishing entitlement to fees, determining the amount of fees and on any appeal. While no litigation, other than to enforce an arbitration award, is contemplated by this Agreement, if there should be litigation arising out of this Agreement the venue for such litigation will be Volusia County, Florida and the law of Florida will apply.

**Severance:** If one or more provisions of this Agreement are held to be unenforceable under applicable law, the parties agree to renegotiate such provision in good faith. Should the parties fail to reach a mutually agreeable and enforceable replacement for such provision, then (a) such provision shall be excluded from this Agreement, (b) the balance of the Agreement shall be interpreted as if such provision were so excluded and (c) the balance of the Agreement shall be enforceable in accordance with its terms.

**Nature of Relationship:** It is not intended, nor should anything contained in this Agreement be construed to constitute that Rising Orchards and Receiving Party are partners, joint venturers, co-owners, or otherwise as participants in a joint or common undertaking. The relation contemplated is contractual, and any new products designed or developed are and shall remain the exclusive property of Rising Orchards unless otherwise agreed in writing by both Parties.

**Affiliates and Assignment:** Except as otherwise indicated in this Agreement, "Rising Orchards" also includes all affiliates of Rising Orchards, and, except as otherwise indicated, the term "Receiving Party" also includes all affiliates of the Receiving Party. An "affiliate" shall mean any person, partnership, joint venture, corporation or other form of enterprise, domestic or foreign, including but not limited to subsidiaries, that directly or indirectly, control or are controlled by, or are under common control with a party. Rising Orchards may assign its rights under this Agreement at its discretion.

**Applicable Law and Venue:** This Agreement is international in scope and applies worldwide. It shall be construed, interpreted and enforced in accordance with and shall be governed by the laws of the State of Florida and the federal laws of the United States without regard to the principles of conflicts of laws.

**Amendment and Waiver:** Any term of this Agreement may be amended with the written consent of both parties. Failure to enforce any provision of this Agreement by a party shall not constitute a waiver of any term hereof by such party.

**Binding Effect:** This Agreement shall bind and inure to the benefit of the parties and their heirs, legal representatives and successors. Any amendment or waiver effected in accordance with this Paragraph shall be binding upon the parties and their respective successors and assigns.

**Entire Agreement:** This Agreement is the product of both of the parties hereto and constitutes the entire agreement between such parties pertaining to the subject matter hereof, and merges all prior negotiations and drafts of the parties with regard to the transactions contemplated herein. Any and all other written or oral agreements existing between the parties hereto regarding such transactions are expressly canceled.
