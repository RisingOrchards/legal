# Cookie Policy

**Effective Date:** June 21, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Rising Orchards LLC d/b/a IgorBox ("IgorBox", "we", "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on our websites and services.

---

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device that help websites function, remember your preferences, and understand how you use the site. We also use similar technologies like pixels, local storage, and SDKs.

---

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

- Strictly necessary: Required for core functionality (e.g., login, session management, security).
- Functional: Remember choices and preferences (e.g., language, region).
- Performance/Analytics: Help us understand usage and improve our services (e.g., page performance, feature adoption, and session recording/replay used for debugging and observability).
- Advertising/Marketing: Measure campaigns and, where applicable, tailor content. We do not enable cross‑context behavioral advertising without consent where required.

---

## 3. Cookies We Set

Examples include:

- Session/authentication cookies (first‑party): maintain your login session and security tokens.
- Preference cookies (first‑party): language, UI settings.
- Analytics cookies (first‑party or third‑party): usage metrics to improve features.

Specific cookies and lifetimes may change over time. Where required by law, we maintain a current cookie list in our consent tool.

---

## 4. Your Choices

- Cookie banner & preferences: On your first visit (and periodically thereafter), you can accept, reject, or manage non‑essential cookies via our preferences tool.
- Global Privacy Control (GPC): If your browser sends a GPC signal, we treat it as an opt‑out of sale/sharing and certain targeted advertising.
- Browser settings: You can block or delete cookies through your browser. If you block strictly necessary cookies, the site may not function properly.

---

## 5. Legal Basis (EEA/UK)

- We use strictly necessary cookies based on our legitimate interest in providing a secure, functional service.
- We use non‑essential cookies (e.g., analytics/advertising) based on your consent. You can withdraw consent at any time via the preferences tool.
