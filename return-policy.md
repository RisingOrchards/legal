# 30-Day Return Policy

**Effective Date:** April 19, 2026
This Return Policy applies to purchases made directly from **IgorBox.com** or authorized IgorBox resellers.

---

## 1. Eligibility for Returns

We want you to be completely satisfied with your IgorBox purchase. If you are not, you may request a return within **30 calendar days** from the date of delivery, subject to the conditions below.

To be eligible for a return:

- The product must be in **like-new** condition, with no damage, wear, or missing parts.
- All original packaging, documentation, and accessories must be included.
- Proof of purchase is required (order number or receipt).

---

## 2. Seasonal Use and "Unofficial Rentals"

IgorBox products are designed for long-term ownership.  
Because many of our customers use our products for seasonal applications, we **do not accept returns for products that show signs of extended seasonal use**.

Examples of non-eligible seasonal returns include (but are not limited to):

- Evidence of multiple months of use.
- Obvious wear, dirt, discoloration, or weather exposure.
- Firmware usage logs showing prolonged operation.
- Packaging or accessories missing or damaged from extended setup.

If a return appears to be an **“unofficial rental”** (purchase, use for the season, then return), the return will be **rejected** or a **restocking fee of up to 50%** may be applied.

---

## 3. Non-Returnable Items

- Software licenses, activation codes, or subscriptions once activated.
- Custom-built or special-order products.
- Clearance, final sale, or “as-is” items.
- Products damaged through misuse, negligence, or modification (including installation of unauthorized firmware).

---

## 4. Return Process

1. **Contact Us** – Email help@igorbox.com with your order number, product details, and reason for return.
2. **Return Authorization** – If eligible, we will issue an RMA (Return Merchandise Authorization) and provide return instructions.
3. **Ship Product Back** – Ship the item in its original packaging with all accessories. You are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error.
4. **Inspection and Refund** – We will inspect the product upon receipt. If approved, a refund will be issued to the original payment method within **10 business days**. Deductions may apply for missing parts, damage, or excessive wear.

---

## 5. Restocking Fees

We reserve the right to apply a restocking fee of **up to 50%** for:

- Items returned with missing parts or damaged packaging.
- Products showing signs of extended seasonal use or unofficial rental.

---

## 6. Exchanges

We will exchange defective products within **30 days** of delivery. Exchanges are subject to product availability.
